Git Setup : For Easy Use & Administration

   Tired of creating that tedious .gitignore file for every project?  Well its .gitignore_global to the rescue!  

   OS and IDE generated files such as, . DS_Store , Thumbs.db and even generated folders like .idea/ , dependency modules, .dll files and logs... can and should be omited from your repos to help keep things simple and clean.

   Here is a very good resource link to help you get your global .gitignore file setup.  Digital Fortress: Create a Global gitignore 

   Need a little help creating your .gitignore_global file?  Here is a really useful online app for that. gitignore.io

   (Tip:  Your .gitignore_global file for windows should reside in the root of your user/<username> folder.  Click here for a sample of this file.)


    With the above setup you can now relax knowing that you are not pushing files with sensitive or needless data to your repos!


   With that all setup, lets get to the ease of administration of your files.  I found TortoiseGit to be extemely useful!

(Note: TortoiseGit will add commands to your right click context menu in windows explorer!)
